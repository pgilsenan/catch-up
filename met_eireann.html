<h1>Current Forecast:</h1> <span class="orangetext">Monday, 10 July 2017 16:47</span>
<hr />

<span class="daybox"> Today</span>
<p>Some dry spells this evening, but rain and drizzle will continue to spread in from the Atlantic. Mild and misty tonight. Mainly dry over Ulster, with just a few patches of drizzle. But becoming damp elsewhere, with outbreaks of rain, the rain, persistent and heavy in parts of south Munster. Patches of fog forming also. Lowest temperatures of 8 to 12 C., in light variable breezes.</p>
